Strawberry Shortcake Milkshake

Total Time : 10 minutes

Ingredients

for 2 milkshakes

  • 1 Â½ cups strawberries, stems removed
  • 1 cup vanilla wafer
  • 2 fl oz brandy, or rum
  • 2 cups strawberry ice cream
  • whipped cream, for topping

Directions

  1. Combine the strawberries, vanilla wafers, rum, and strawberry ice cream in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Top with whipped cream and strawberries.
  3. Enjoy!
