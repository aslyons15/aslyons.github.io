Marshmallow Smores

Total Time : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • Graham crackers
  • 2 chocolate bars (milk chocolate or dark chocolate)
  • marshmallows (standard size)

Directions

  1. Break the graham crackers along the scored line so you have 2 pieces.
  2. Place your marshmallow on the cracker and microwave for 30 seconds
  3. After done put chocolate bar on top of marshmallow and close it with the other piece
  4. Enjoy!
