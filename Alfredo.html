Fettuccini Alfredo

Total Time

5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups milk (works with 1% or 2% low fat or whole milk)
  • 3 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instrutions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk milk & flour until smooth.
  2. Add Parmesan and salt.
  3. Place in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat. Cook and stir over low heat until thickened, about 2 minutes.
  5. Serve over fettuccine or your favorite pasta.
  6. Enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories 250g
  • Fat 7g
  • Carbs 38g
  • Fiber 6.9g
  • Sugar 2g
  • Protein 10g
